Breaking Bad Home The Sopranos

Breaking Bad is a captivating drama about a high school chemistry teacher, Walter White, who's life changes as he is diagnosed with terminal lung cancer. On a ride-along with his brother-in-law, a DEA agent, Walt sees how "easy" it is to make money selling meth, and decides that if he is to die in a few short months, he may as well leave his family enough money to survive comfortably while he is gone. The show then follows Walt through a thrilling 5 seasons as he fights through cancer and morphs from an innocent family man to a nefarious drug kingpin.
