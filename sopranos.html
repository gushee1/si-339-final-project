Breaking Bad Home The Sopranos

Tony Soprano is a the head of the notorious Soprano crime family in New Jersey, but this show focuses less on the day to day operations of the mob, and more on the character of Tony himself. A surprising take at the time, but one that has been replicated countless times since, The Sopranos paints Tony as a flawed antihero, who viewers can't help but sympathize with, even after witnessing firsthand the awful things he does. Over the course of 6 seasons, viewers see Tony fight through past trauma in therapy, deal with the coming of age of his children, and handle the changing dynamics of his mob family.
