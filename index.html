Will's Favorite TV Shows
Breaking Bad Home The Sopranos

In my sophomore year of high school, my chemistry teacher had a Breaking Bad poster on his classroom wall. He would always inject anecdotes from the show into our class, so I decided to watch it on his recommendation. I immediately was hooked, and finished the show within a few months. The whole time I watched it, I had heard of how heavily it draws inspiration from The Sopranos, but had never though of watching it.

Read an article that makes some references to the similarities between Tony and Walt.

However, a few years later, during quarantine, my family decided to sit down and watch The Sopranos, as my parents believe it is a necessary part of cultural literacy. Now, after watching both, I see many of the parallels that are talked about between the two shows, and can confidently say they are two of my favorite shows of all time.

Juan Bolsa (left) translated to English is John Sack, referencing The Sopranos character John Sacrimoni (right), or "Johnny Sack"
